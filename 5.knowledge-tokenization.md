## 🔍 Overview
Conceptual framework: knowledge as non-fungible value (NOT cryptocurrency).

### Principles
- 🎓 Knowledge as contribution, not commodity
- 🔗 Attribution via DOI/ORCID, not financial tokens
- 🌱 Value measured by impact, not price
